Our Story

Founded in 2016, DC416 evolved from informal gatherings in local tech spaces into one of Canada's most respected cybersecurity communities. Today we host regular meetups, workshops, and collaborative projects that unite students, researchers, and professionals across the Greater Toronto Area.

Our community remains strictly vendor neutral and driven by its members. We believe in the power of shared knowledge to create a space where everyone from beginners to industry veterans can advance the field of security together.

Our Mission

To foster an inclusive, educational cybersecurity community in Toronto that promotes knowledge sharing, skill development, and ethical practices through collaborative learning experiences.

Our Vision

To be a catalyst for cybersecurity innovation and education in Toronto, empowering individuals to protect digital assets and contribute positively to the global security landscape.
