Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join DEFCON Toronto?

Just show up. We meet monthly (usually the 3rd Thursday). Events are free, but capacity is limited, so you must RSVP on our event page. Between events, join the conversation on our Discord.

Are your events only for experienced professionals?

No. We welcome everyone—from students and skiddies to red team veterans. You don’t need a PhD or a job title, just a genuine interest in security. Leave the ego at the door.

How can I present at one of your events?

Pitch us on Discord or grab an organizer at an event. We want technical deep dives, live demos, and war stories. The only hard rule: No vendor pitches. If you are trying to sell a product, don't apply.

Is DC416 officially affiliated with DEFCON?

Yes. We are a registered DEF CON Group. We operate under the official charter to bring the raw, technical spirit of the Las Vegas conference to Toronto.
