Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join DEFCON Toronto?

There's no formal membership process. Simply attend our events, join our Discord server, and participate in our online and offline activities. All skill levels are welcome!

Are your events only for experienced professionals?

Not at all! We welcome attendees of all skill levels, from beginners to experts. Many of our events include introductory content and mentorship opportunities for those new to cybersecurity.

How can I present at one of your events?

We're always looking for speakers! Contact us through this form with your topic idea, a brief overview of your presentation, and your availability. Our team will get back to you to discuss details.

Is DC416 officially affiliated with DEFCON?

DC416 is a recognized DEFCON Group (DCG), which means we're part of the global community of local groups inspired by the DEFCON conference. We operate independently but adhere to the spirit and principles of the wider DEFCON community.
